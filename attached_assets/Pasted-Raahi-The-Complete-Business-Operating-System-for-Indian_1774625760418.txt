Raahi — The Complete Business Operating System for Indian Startups & SMBs
One Line: "From idea to operation — every legal, regulatory, financial, and administrative thing your business needs, in one place."

The Core Insight
When someone starts a business in India, they face 5 distinct categories of pain — and right now, they need 5 different vendors, consultants, or government portals to solve each one. Nobody has stitched them together.
Pain #1 → "What licenses do I even need?"         → No one answers this clearly
Pain #2 → "Am I filing everything correctly?"      → CAs, but expensive & slow
Pain #3 → "Am I following labour laws?"            → Nobody tracks this for SMBs
Pain #4 → "What government benefits am I missing?" → Zero awareness
Pain #5 → "Are my contracts & IP protected?"       → Lawyers, but costly
You solve all 5. In one dashboard.

💳 PILLAR 6 — Compliance Credit Score
The long-term play that makes this a platform, not just a tool.
This is the feature that makes the product genuinely unique in the world — not just India.
What it does:
Based on all their data — GST filings, MCA compliance, labour law adherence, license renewals — you generate a Compliance Credit Score (say, 0-850).
Why it matters:

Banks and NBFCs currently have no way to assess the "regulatory health" of an SMB
A high compliance score = lower lending risk
You become a credit data provider to lenders

Business implications:

Lenders pay YOU for access to compliance scores
Businesses with high scores get pre-approved loan offers inside your platform
Insurance companies use it for premium pricing
Government procurement platforms (GeM) can use it for vendor trust

This turns you from a SaaS tool into a financial data platform. That's where the real valuation is.
